Work History Overview

LCT “NAVISTAR”

Works Carried Out:

  • Cropped out and renewed 72 sheets of MS Plates at the main deck
  • Fabrication and installation of lashing eye at the main deck
  • Cropping out and renewal of deteriorated stanchion post
  • Renewal of 32 pcs flush type manholes
  • Renewal of air vents and filling pipes (P/S)
  • Carpentry works at crew quarters accommodation.
  • Installed and Fabricated watertight doors & weathertight doors.
  • Cropping out and Renewal of Deteriorated side shell plating & bulwark plating (P/S).
  • Installation and fabrication of sewage tank and piping as per MARINA Regulation Requirements.

  • Fabrication and Installation of compartment breather at the main deck (P/S)
  • Fabrication and Installation of compartment filling pipe at the Main deck.
  • Recondition of 16 pcs. Globe Valve
  • Fabrication and Installation of 26 sets of stanchion post

  • Fabrication and Installation of pad eye at the bow fwd (P/S)
  • Recondition of additional 6 pcs Globe Valve
  • Re-framing of ballast tank #2 (Stbd side) and Ballast tank #5 (P/S)
  • Fabrication and Installation of 116 pcs lashing pad eye
  • Welding 27 Pinholes at Bottom plates and welding crack at the skeg
