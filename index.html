About Us

BCT Ships Repair Services is a ships’ repair contractor Serving Shipyards and Commercial Ship-Owners in the Philippine Maritime Sector. Our office is located at Pag-Utlan, Tayud, Consolacion, Cebu, Philippines.

We have earned our well-regarded reputation by providing an accurate job, reasonable pricing, guaranteed reliability, quality standard results with economical cost of ship’s repair services to our clients. We are equipped and highly qualified to provide Engineering Services as well as Structural and Machinery repairs.

BCT’s quality assurance and safety standard is already recognized by MARITIME INDUSTRY AUTHORITY (MARINA). With a can-do attitude, our customers could immediately benefit from our experienced team of ship repair professionals.

Commitment
