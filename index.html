About Us

BCT Ships Repair Services is a ships’ repair contractor serving shipyards and commercial ship-owners in the Philippine maritime sector. Our office is located at Pag-Utlan, Tayud, Consolacion, Cebu, Philippines.

We have earned our well-regarded reputation by providing accurate job performance, reasonable pricing, guaranteed reliability, and quality standard results with economical costs of ship repair services to our clients. We are equipped and highly qualified to provide engineering services as well as structural and machinery repairs.

BCT’s quality assurance and safety standard is recognized by the Maritime Industry Authority (MARINA). With a can-do attitude, our customers immediately benefit from our experienced team of ship repair professionals.

Commitment

We bond a strong relationship with all our clients, supporting them wherever they are, and we will meet their needs to provide them with the best repair services possible.

Mission

Our mission is to provide superior ship repair services, prioritizing the best interests of both our customers and our dedicated employees. We strive for excellence in customer service through continuous development of our craftsmanship, competencies, and a positive, results-driven approach. Building productive, customer-centric, and service-oriented relationships, we are also committed to enhancing the value of our services through proactive environmental protection.

Vision

“To be one of the leading local ship repair service providers in the Philippine maritime industry.”
